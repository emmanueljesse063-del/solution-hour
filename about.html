About Solution Hour International Centre

Solution Hour International Centre is a Spirit-filled ministry with a deep passion for reaching souls, raising champions, and building people for Kingdom purpose. Our church is a family of believers rooted in the Word and led by the Spirit.

Since our foundation, we have been committed to transforming lives through worship, teaching, prayer, and outreach. We believe that God has called us to be a light to the nations — bringing healing, hope, and salvation to all who encounter His presence.

Our Vision

To raise champions for Christ and influence nations through the transforming power of God’s Word.

Our Mission

To equip believers for a victorious Christian life, strengthen families, and impact the world through faith, service, and love.

Our Core Values

  • Faith and the Word
  • Integrity and Excellence
  • Love and Compassion
  • Service and Leadership
